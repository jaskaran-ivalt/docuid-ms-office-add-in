Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to DocuID. This Privacy Policy specifically explains how the DocuID add-in for MS Office ("App", "Service", or "Add-in") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services within Microsoft Office applications.

Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy clearly outlines our operations concerning the "DocuID add-in for MS Office" service overall, not just the DocuID main website.

2. Information Regarding User's Personal Information

When utilizing the DocuID add-in for MS Office, we limit the collection of your personal information to the strict minimum necessary to provide you with secure document management functionalities.

  • Account Information: We securely authenticate you using our DocuID backend to ensure you have the correct permissions to interact with or secure the active document.
  • Document Data: The Add-in reads or writes only to the active document you have explicitly chosen to process. Document contents are strictly processed per your requests to provide the Add-in's core functionality.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personally identifiable telemetry to monitor the Add-in's performance and crash reports, exclusively used to improve product stability.

3. How We Use Information

We process your information strictly for the following purposes:

  • To operate and provide the core features of the DocuID add-in for MS Office.
  • To authenticate you securely and verify permissions against the DocuID network.
  • To provide customer support and respond to technical issues.

4. Data Security

We implement stringently audited security measures to ensure that your personal information and documents are not lost, misused, accessed, altered, or destroyed improperly. All communication between the Add-in and our servers uses industry-standard HTTPS encryption. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Third-Party Access

The DocuID add-in for MS Office strictly communicates with Microsoft Office APIs (to read/write the active document context) and DocuID's secure backend servers. We do not share your document contents or personal information with unrelated marketing third parties.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, your personal information, or the DocuID add-in for MS Office, please reach out to our dedicated privacy support.
