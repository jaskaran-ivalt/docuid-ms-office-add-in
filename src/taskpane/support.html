Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I access the DocuID Add-in?

Once installed from the Office Store, you can access the DocuID MS Office Add-in by clicking on its icon in the Home ribbon of Microsoft Word or other supported applications. A side panel will open where you can sign in to your DocuID account.

I'm receiving a connection error. What do I do?

Ensure that your internet connection is active. The Add-in requires a secure connection to DocuID servers. If you're on a corporate network, ensure your firewall is not blocking access to *.docuid.net.

Is the Add-in free?

The Add-in is free to download and install, but it requires a valid and active DocuID account to function properly. Please visit our website for pricing details.
