# DocuID Platform — Progress & Roadmap

> Product Progress Briefing — May 2026

A secure, identity-verified document platform — giving organisations full control over who accesses their files, with every interaction tied to a verified identity.

- [docuid.net](https://docuid.net)
- [Microsoft AppSource](https://marketplace.microsoft.com/en-us/product/WA200010668?tab=Overview)

---

## The Platform

**Two products. One verified identity.**

DocuID is built on iVALT's Human-Bound Identity platform. Every file access is tied to a real, verified person — not a password, not a shared link.

### docuid.net
The web platform — upload, organise, and share documents securely in the cloud. All access requires biometric verification.

### Microsoft Office Add-in
Access your DocuID documents from inside Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint — verified by biometrics at every step.

## Phase 3 — Planned

**Maximum security for every document.**

We are upgrading to the highest level of document protection — where files are secured on your own device before they ever reach the cloud.

- **Your device protects the file first** — Before any document is uploaded, it is fully encrypted on your own device. What is stored in the cloud is already secured — only authorised users can open it. Your documents remain protected even if the cloud storage were ever compromised.
- **Access control** — Each document has its own unique key. Only you and the people you explicitly authorise can ever open it.
- **Per-file protection** — Every document gets its own independent protection — isolating files ensures a single issue cannot affect your entire library.

## Phase 4 — Planned

**Global document signing for verified authenticity.**

We are building a document signing layer directly into DocuID — giving every signed document a verifiable, identity-bound signature backed by iVALT biometrics. No third-party signing service needed.

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Sign** | Every signature is tied to the signer's verified iVALT identity — not just a typed name or image. |
| **Verify** | Anyone can verify a document's signature and confirm it has not been altered since it was signed. |
| **Send** | Send documents to anyone worldwide for signing. Recipients verify their identity and sign through iVALT. |
| **Audit trail** | A complete, tamper-proof record of who signed, when, and from where — attached to every document. |
| **Compliance-ready** | Signatures meet the requirements of major legal frameworks — built for cross-border and enterprise use. |
| **Native to DocuID** | Signing is built into the same platform — no switching between tools, no third-party accounts required. |
