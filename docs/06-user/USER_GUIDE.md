# DocuID User Guide

## 1. Welcome to DocuID

Welcome! DocuID provides a secure and easy way to access your sensitive documents directly within Microsoft Office. This guide will walk you through setting up and using the DocuID add-in.

## 2. Getting Started: Installation

The DocuID add-in must be installed by your organization's Office 365 administrator. Once installed, it will automatically appear in your Office applications (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).

You can find it in the "Home" tab of the Office ribbon.

## 3. How to Use DocuID

Using DocuID is a simple, two-step process:

### Step 1: Open the Add-in and Enter Your Mobile Number

1.  Click the **DocuID** button in the Office ribbon to open the task pane.
2.  You will see a welcome screen asking for your registered mobile number.
3.  Select your country code from the dropdown list.
4.  Enter the mobile number associated with your account.
5.  Click the **"Login with Biometrics"** button.

![Login Screen](https://via.placeholder.com/300x200.png?text=Login+Screen)

_A push notification will be sent to your mobile device._

### Step 2: Authenticate on Your Mobile Device

1.  Open the notification on your phone.
2.  You will be prompted to verify your identity using your device's biometric sensor (e.g., fingerprint or face scan).
3.  Once you successfully authenticate, the DocuID add-in on your desktop will automatically unlock.

![Mobile Authentication](https://via.placeholder.com/150x300.png?text=Mobile+Auth)

## 4. Accessing Your Documents

After successful authentication, you will see a list of your available documents.

- **Search**: You can use the search bar at the top to quickly find a document by its title.
- **Open**: Click the **"Open"** button next to a document to open it directly in the current Office application.

![Document List](https://via.placeholder.com/300x400.png?text=Document+List)

## 5. Troubleshooting

### Issue: Push notification not received.

- **Solution 1**: Ensure your mobile device has a stable internet connection.
- **Solution 2**: Check your device's notification settings to make sure you have allowed notifications from the DocuID authenticator app.
- **Solution 3**: Try closing and reopening the DocuID task pane and attempting to log in again.

### Issue: Biometric authentication fails.

- **Solution 1**: Make sure your finger or face is positioned correctly for the sensor.
- **Solution 2**: Ensure your device's biometric settings are configured correctly.

### Issue: "No Documents Found" message appears.

- This means that either no documents have been shared with you or your search query does not match any document titles.
- Try clearing your search term or contact your administrator to ensure you have the correct permissions.

## 6. Security

Your security is our top priority.

- Your biometric data never leaves your mobile device.
- All communication between the add-in, our servers, and your device is encrypted using industry-standard protocols.

For further assistance, please contact your IT support desk.
