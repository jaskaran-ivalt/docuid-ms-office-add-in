Phase 3
Planned

Maximum security
for every document.

We are upgrading to the highest level of document protection — where files are secured on your own device before they ever reach the cloud.

Your device protects the file first
Before any document is uploaded, it is fully encrypted on your own device. What is stored in the cloud is already secured — only authorised users can open it. Your documents remain protected even if the cloud storage were ever compromised.
Access control
Each document has its own unique key. Only you and the people you explicitly authorise can ever open it.
Per-file protection
Every document gets its own independent protection — isolating files ensures a single issue cannot affect your entire library.
Phase 4
Planned

Global document signing
for verified authenticity.

We are building a document signing layer directly into DocuID — giving every signed document a verifiable, identity-bound signature backed by iVALT biometrics. No third-party signing service needed.

Sign
Biometric-bound signatures
Every signature is tied to the signer's verified iVALT identity — not just a typed name or image.
Verify
Instant authenticity check
Anyone can verify a document's signature and confirm it has not been altered since it was signed.
Send
Request signatures globally
Send documents to anyone worldwide for signing. Recipients verify their identity and sign through iVALT.
Audit trail
A complete, tamper-proof record of who signed, when, and from where — attached to every document.
Compliance-ready
Signatures meet the requirements of major legal frameworks — built for cross-border and enterprise use.
Native to DocuID
Signing is built into the same platform — no switching between tools, no third-party accounts required.
